Account

Delete your account

You can permanently delete your Blakey Trades account and all associated data at any time.

Option 1 — In the app (fastest)

  1. Open Blakey Trades and go to Settings → Account & security
  2. Scroll down and tap Delete account
  3. Confirm — this takes effect immediately

Option 2 — By email

If you can't access the app, email kyle.airey@hotmail.com from the address linked to your account, with the subject "Delete my account". We'll verify your identity and complete the deletion within 30 days, in line with UK GDPR.

What gets deleted: your login credentials, profile, private trading journal, progress/streaks, community messages, and direct messages — permanently and irreversibly. Messages you sent to other members may remain visible to them as part of their own conversation history, consistent with how messaging works in any app.

What we keep

We don't retain a copy of your personal data after deletion, except where we're required to by law (for example, financial or fraud-prevention records, kept only as long as legally mandated).

Questions about deletion or your data: kyle.airey@hotmail.com — see also our Privacy Policy.
