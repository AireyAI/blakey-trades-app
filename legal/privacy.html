Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 July 2026 · Applies to the Blakey Trades app and blakeytrades.com

In short: we collect the minimum needed to run your account, your journal, and the community floor — we never sell your data, we don't access your broker account or trading activity, and you can delete everything permanently at any time.

1. Who we are

Blakey Trades is operated by Kyle Airey, a sole trader based in England, trading as Blakey Trades ("we", "us", "our"). For the purposes of UK GDPR, Kyle Airey is the data controller for information collected through the app and this website.

Contact: kyle.airey@hotmail.com

3. What we do not collect

We never ask for, store, or have access to your broker password, trading account balance, open positions, or trade execution history. Linking a broker account number for VIP verification does not give us — or the app — any ability to view, place, or manage trades on your behalf.

4. Broker partnership (Vantage)

Blakey Trades VIP is funded through a partnership with the broker Vantage. If you choose to link an account, we compare the account number you provide against a list supplied to us by Vantage to confirm it was opened under our partnership. We do not send your personal data to Vantage, and we do not receive your trading activity from them — only confirmation of whether an account number is linked to our partnership.

5. Who we share data with

  • Supabase — our database, authentication, and backend infrastructure provider (data processor). Supabase stores your account and app data on our behalf.
  • Expo — delivers push notifications to your device; receives your device push token only.
  • Apple / Google — if you sign in with Apple or Google, they act as your identity provider under their own privacy terms.

We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with advertisers or data brokers.

6. How long we keep it

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account (see our Account Deletion page), your login, journal, progress, and messages are permanently and irreversibly removed.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data (editable in-app under Settings → Profile)
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time, permanently
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk) if you believe we've mishandled your data

To exercise any of these rights, email kyle.airey@hotmail.com.

8. Children

Blakey Trades is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. Financial market content and community chat are not appropriate for minors.

9. Security

Access to your data is protected by row-level security at the database layer — your journal and private messages are only ever readable by you. We use industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS).

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated date; continued use of the app after a change means you accept the update.

11. Contact
