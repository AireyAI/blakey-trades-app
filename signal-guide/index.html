Signal Integration Guide

How your signals reach the app

This explains exactly how the trade signals you post in Telegram appear inside the Blakey Trades app — automatically, in seconds, for your verified members. Nothing changes about how you work. You keep posting in Telegram exactly as you do now; the app reads your calls and delivers them. You never post in two places.

The one thing we need from you: add our app's bot — @Blakeytradesappbot — as an administrator on each of your signal channels. It takes about 30 seconds per channel. That's the only action required on your side — everything after that is automatic. Full steps are in Section 2.

1How it works

From the moment you hit send in Telegram to a notification on your member's phone — in seconds.

1
You post a signal in Telegram

Exactly as you do today — entry, stop, targets — in your VIP, Swing, Zones or Scalps channels. No new tools, no new steps.

2
Our bot reads the post instantly

A private bot — created and managed entirely by us — sits quietly in your channels as an admin. The instant you post, it receives the message. Your members never see it; it doesn't post anything.

3
The signal is read and structured

The system reads your call and pulls out the details — pair, direction, entry, stop and every target — and files them as a clean, tappable trade in the app.

4
It's delivered to verified members

The trade appears live in the app's signal feed and a push notification lands on every verified member's phone — typically within a few seconds of you posting.

5
Your follow-ups keep it updated

When you post an update — "partials here", "stop to breakeven", "closed +40 pips" — the app updates the same trade in place, so members always see its current state, not a cluttered stream.

2What we need from you

Add our bot as an administrator on each signal channel. The bot to add is:

📡
@Blakeytradesappbot
Search this exact name when adding an administrator.

In Telegram:

  1. Open the channel in Telegram and tap the channel name at the top.
  2. Tap Administrators (under "Manage Channel" on some versions).
  3. Tap Add Administrator and search @Blakeytradesappbot.
  4. Select it and tap Save/Done. It only needs to read posts — you can leave every other permission off.
  5. Repeat for each signal channel you want feeding the app.
Is this safe? Being an admin is simply what Telegram requires for a bot to receive a channel's posts — it does not give anyone control of your channel, and your subscribers won't see any change. You can remove the bot at any time.

3What your members see

  • Your entry, stop and targets become a full trade card — live chart, levels, and status (running / hit target / stopped).
  • Follow-ups — "partials", "SL to breakeven", "closed" — update that same card, not a cluttered stream of new messages.
  • Only verified members (confirmed under your broker partnership) see the entry, stop and targets. Anyone else sees a call is running but stays locked out — with a prompt to get verified.
Example. You post in Telegram:
🔴 SELL XAUUSD — Entry 4,051, SL 4,059, TP1 4,043 · TP2 4,035
Seconds later every verified member gets a push — "🔴 BT VIP · New gold call" — and taps into a full trade card with the chart and levels already laid out.
The ask, in short: add @Blakeytradesappbot as an administrator on each signal channel you want feeding the app (Section 2), and confirm which channels those are. That's it — your signals start flowing automatically. Any questions, we're happy to walk through it live.
