Signal Integration Guide

How your signals reach the app

This explains exactly how the trade signals you post in Telegram appear inside the Blakey Trades app — automatically, in seconds, for your verified members. Nothing changes about how you work. You keep posting in Telegram exactly as you do now; the app reads your calls and delivers them. You never post in two places.

The one thing we need from you: add our app's bot as an administrator on each of your signal channels. It takes about 30 seconds per channel. That's the only action required on your side — everything after that is automatic. Full steps are in Section 2.

1How it works

From the moment you hit send in Telegram to a notification on your member's phone — in seconds.

1
You post a signal in Telegram

Exactly as you do today — entry, stop, targets — in your VIP, Swing, Zones or Scalps channels. No new tools, no new steps.

2
Our bot reads the post instantly

A private bot — created and managed entirely by us — sits quietly in your channels as an admin. The instant you post, it receives the message. Your members never see it; it doesn't post anything.

3
The signal is read and structured

The system reads your call and pulls out the details — pair, direction, entry, stop and every target — and files them as a clean, tappable trade in the app.

4
It's delivered to verified members

The trade appears live in the app's signal feed and a push notification lands on every verified member's phone — typically within a few seconds of you posting.

5
Your follow-ups keep it updated

When you post an update — "partials here", "stop to breakeven", "closed +40 pips" — the app updates the same trade in place, so members always see its current state, not a cluttered stream.

2What we need from you

Add our bot as an administrator on each signal channel. We'll send you the bot's exact username; then, in Telegram:

  1. Open the channel in Telegram and tap the channel name at the top.
  2. Tap Administrators (under "Manage Channel" on some versions).
  3. Tap Add Administrator and search the bot username we give you.
  4. Select it and tap Save/Done. It only needs to read posts — you can leave every other permission off.
  5. Repeat for each signal channel you want feeding the app.
Is this safe? Being an admin is simply what Telegram requires for a bot to receive a channel's posts — it does not give anyone control of your channel, and your subscribers won't see any change. You can remove the bot at any time.

3What happens to each signal

Entry signals

Read cleanly, every time

Your entry, stop and all targets are pulled out and shown as a proper trade card — with a live chart, the levels, and its status (running / hit target / stopped).

Follow-up updates

Attached to the right trade

"Partials", "SL to breakeven", "closed" — these attach to the original call and update its status, rather than appearing as loose, confusing messages.

Edits

Kept in sync

If you edit a message in Telegram, the app updates that same trade — so what members see always matches what you posted.

Education & chat

Stays where it belongs

Only your trade calls are turned into signals. General chat and education aren't mistaken for trades — the system knows the difference.

Example. You post in Telegram:
🔴 SELL XAUUSD — Entry 4,051, SL 4,059, TP1 4,043 · TP2 4,035
Seconds later every verified member gets a push — "🔴 BT VIP · New gold call" — and taps into a full trade card with the chart and levels already laid out.

4Who receives the signals

Your calls only go to the members who should have them.

Every signal is delivered only to verified members — the traders confirmed under your broker partnership. Anyone not verified sees that a live call is running but the entry, stop and targets stay locked, with a prompt to get verified. So the app doesn't just deliver your signals faster than Telegram — it makes sure they reach the right people, and turns the lock screen into a route for new members to join properly.

5Your workflow — unchanged

  • You post once, in Telegram, exactly as you do now.
  • No new app, dashboard or tool to learn — you change nothing.
  • The app becomes the premium, members-only home for your calls — with push alerts, a verified track record, and everything gated to your members.
  • You can add or remove the bot from any channel whenever you like — you stay in full control.

6Summary — the ask

Everything we need to switch this on:

  • Admin access for our bot on each signal channel (we send you the username; ~30 seconds per channel — Section 2).
  • Confirm which channels should feed the app.

That's it. Once the bot is an admin on your channels, your signals start flowing into the app automatically. Any questions, we're happy to walk through it live.
